# Company History

- **2020** – Metalogics was founded with a vision to offer modern web development services.
- **2021** – Expanded into mobile app development, serving startups and SMEs globally.
- **2022** – Introduced blockchain-based solutions and decentralized application development.
- **2023** – Launched AI-powered solutions, integrating machine learning and automation into client projects.
- **2024–Present** – Growing as a full-service digital partner, offering tailored strategies for businesses worldwide.
